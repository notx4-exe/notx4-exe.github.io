Our Story

About Vidya Bharati

A legacy of 35 years, shaping generations of brilliant minds

Our History

Founded in 1989

Vidya Bharati School of Excellence was established in 1989 with a vision to provide world-class education in a nurturing environment. What started as a small institution with 200 students has grown into a premier educational establishment with over 2,500 students across Classes 1 to 12.

Our journey spans three decades of academic achievement, sports victories, cultural milestones, and above all, the transformation of students into confident, capable individuals ready to face the world.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted centre of learning that develops holistically educated global citizens who are rooted in values, passionate about knowledge, and committed to serving society.

Our Mission

To provide quality education with a blend of modern technology and timeless values, nurturing intellectual curiosity, physical fitness, and moral integrity in every student.
