Our Story

Established in 1995, Thico Silks Matric Hr Sec School stands as a beacon of academic excellence in Thirubuvanam. Over three decades, we have nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to excel in engineering, medicine, arts, and public service.

Situated on Main Road, Thirubuvanam, within the historically rich Thiruvidaimaruthur Taluk of Kumbakonam, our campus reflects the cultural depth and scholarly tradition of this sacred region of Tamil Nadu.
